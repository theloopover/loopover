05

Personalised Advertising

We use advertising technologies in our games and exchange ad identifiers with external advertising networks to allow personalised advertising within our games. We and our trusted partners collect and process the Apple Advertising ID (IDFA) on iOS devices and the Google Advertising ID on Android devices to allow interest-based advertising and measure the success of aforementioned advertising.

Advertising IDs are unique but at the same time non-personalised and non-permanent identification numbers provided by your operating system and device.

06

Opting Out of Ad Personalisation

You can limit or disable personalised advertising on your device at any time:

Android

Open the Settings application and select the Google menu item. From there, select the Ads sub-item and activate "Opt out of Ads Personalisation". You can also reset your advertising ID in the same menu at any time.

iOS

Open the Settings application of iOS and select the Privacy menu item and then the Advertising sub-item. Activate "Limit ad tracking" to restrict personalised ads. You can also reset the IDFA in the same menu.

Sharing With Social Media

Our games may offer social sharing features, which let you share the Google Play store link (Android) / Apple App store link (iOS). This provides the link to the respective store via a method of your choice (e.g. a messaging service / social media). We do not collect any information on this.

Our games may also provide a 'Rate App' button which will take you to your respective store to leave a user rating for the game. We do not collect any information on this.
