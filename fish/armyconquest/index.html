HOW TO PLAY

Drag: Press & drag from your territory to any tile on the map.

Pathfinding: Troops automatically route through your hexes. Max 6 hops.

Split: Drag further to send more troops (25% → 100%).

Release: Let go to launch. Combat only happens at the destination.

Waves: Every 40s all factions receive bonus troops.

Combat: Risk-style dice — attacker rolls up to 3, defender up to 2.
